### Description
Zakovat Timer is a professional timer designed specifically for the "Zakovat" intellectual game and similar quiz competitions.

Key features:
• Three modes: Normal, Duplet, and Blitz
• Precise time tracking with audio signals
• Voice countdown ("10 seconds left", "Time's up")
• Extra 10-second answer-writing mode
• Warning signal at 10 seconds remaining
• Haptic (vibration) support
• Dark and light themes
• Screen stays awake while the timer runs
• Background alerts via notifications
• Works offline

The app offers a simple, reliable interface convenient for game hosts and teams.

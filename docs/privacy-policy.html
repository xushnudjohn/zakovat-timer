Zakovat Timer — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026.

Zakovat Timer does not collect, store, or share any personal user data.

Data Collection

The app does not request or collect any personal information (name, email, location, contacts, etc.). All settings are stored locally on your device only.

Internet & Third Parties

The app does not require an internet connection to function. It uses no advertising networks and no analytics services.

Permissions

The app only uses the notification permission to alert you when a timer ends. You can revoke this permission at any time in your device settings.

Children's Privacy

The app is safe for all ages and collects no data from children.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
